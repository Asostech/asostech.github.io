About Us

At OFOET NIG LTD, our primary focus is delivering affordable, high-quality solar renewable energy solutions and materials. We provide a wide range of products and services including power hybrid inverters, smart CCTV cameras, secure access control systems, electrical installations, and facilities management for homeowners and businesses across Nigeria. Whether you have a large family or a high-energy household, we are here to meet your power needs. We are a specialist solar energy company committed to sustainable, reliable, and cost-effective PV solutions. Our team is made up of certified engineers and electricians who design and install systems that comply with both local regulations and international standards.

  • Certified installation teams
  • Warranty-backed equipment
  • Site surveys & custom designs

Why Choose Us

OFOET NIG LTD provides clean, reliable, and cost-saving on-site solar solutions that cut carbon emissions, boost energy security, and help homes, businesses and institutions meet sustainability goals. It also supports local communities through ethical, unionized domestic supply chains and targeted community investments.
